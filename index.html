Who I am

Full-Stack developer based out of NYC

Hi, I'm Mickey! I'm a full-stack software engineer with a background in music performance, creative writing, and education. Over the last ten years I've been focused on growing my drum lessons studio in Brooklyn, which aims to provide a space for women, trans and non-binary folks who want to learn the drums in a queer-friendly, non-competitive environment. As I shift my focus to working in the tech space, my aim is to combine my skills in technology, communication, and education in order to positively impact the world around me.

A creative and curious team player by nature, as well as a highly-trained performer and educator, I always consider the needs of my audience, and am relentlessly driven to create well-rounded, streamlined products that are not only enjoyable, but meaningful to use. In addition to studying performance at Berklee College of Music, I am a graduate of The Evergreen State College (BA in Music Education) and the Software Engineering Immersive at General Assembly.
